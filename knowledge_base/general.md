# General Information — Knowledge Base

## Business Hours & Support Availability

- **Email support**: Available 24/7. Response times:
  - Starter plan: within **24 hours**
  - Pro plan: within **4 hours**
  - Enterprise plan: within **1 hour** (with dedicated account manager)
- **Live chat**: Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM EST.
- **Phone support**: Available for Enterprise plan customers only. Contact your account manager for the direct line.

## Contact Channels

- **Email**: support@example.com
- **Help Centre**: https://help.example.com
- **Community Forum**: https://community.example.com
- **Twitter / X**: @ExampleSupport (for non-sensitive enquiries only)
- **Status Page**: https://status.example.com (for real-time system status and incident updates)

## Service Level Agreements (SLA)

| Severity   | Description                                  | Target Response | Target Resolution |
|------------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Critical   | Service completely down, all users affected   | 15 minutes      | 4 hours           |
| High       | Major feature broken, many users affected     | 1 hour          | 8 hours           |
| Medium     | Feature degraded, workaround available        | 4 hours         | 24 hours          |
| Low        | Minor issue, cosmetic, or feature request     | 24 hours        | Best effort       |

- SLA clock runs during **business hours only** for Starter and Pro plans.
- Enterprise SLA runs **24/7**.

## Feature Requests

- Customers can submit feature requests at **Help Centre → Feature Requests**.
- Feature requests are reviewed by the product team on a monthly basis.
- Popular requests (50+ votes) are fast-tracked for consideration.
- We do **not** provide ETAs for feature requests but customers are notified when a requested feature ships.

## Privacy & Compliance

- We are **SOC 2 Type II** certified and **GDPR** compliant.
- Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
- Customer data is hosted in **AWS US-East-1** (US customers) and **AWS EU-West-1** (EU customers).
- Data Processing Agreements (DPA) are available for Enterprise customers.
- For data subject access requests (DSAR), contact privacy@example.com.

## System Requirements

- **Browsers**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 15+, Edge 90+.
- **Mobile**: iOS 15+ (Safari), Android 12+ (Chrome).
- **API**: REST API with JSON payloads. SDKs available for Python, Node.js, Ruby, and Go.
