# Billing & Payments — Knowledge Base

## Refund Policy

- Customers can request a full refund within **30 days** of purchase.
- After 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis and require manager approval.
- Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within **5-10 business days**.
- Partial refunds are available for annual plans if less than 3 months have been used.

## Double-Charge / Duplicate Billing

- If a customer reports being charged twice, verify the transaction in the billing dashboard.
- Confirm whether one charge is a pre-authorisation hold (these drop off within 3-5 business days).
- If a genuine duplicate charge is confirmed, issue an immediate refund for the extra charge.
- Provide the customer with a refund confirmation number.

## Subscription Plans

| Plan       | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Features                              |
|------------|---------------|--------------|---------------------------------------|
| Starter    | $9/mo         | $89/yr       | 1 user, 5 GB storage, email support   |
| Pro        | $29/mo        | $279/yr      | 5 users, 50 GB storage, priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom        | Custom       | Unlimited users, unlimited storage, dedicated account manager |

- Plan upgrades take effect immediately; the prorated difference is charged.
- Plan downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
- Customers can switch plans at any time from **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**.

## Invoices & Receipts

- Invoices are emailed automatically after each payment.
- Past invoices can be downloaded from **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.
- If a customer needs a corrected invoice (e.g., updated company name or VAT number), they can update their billing details and regenerate the invoice.

## Payment Methods

- Accepted methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfer (Enterprise only).
- To update a payment method: **Settings → Billing → Payment Methods → Update**.
- If a payment fails, the system retries up to 3 times over 7 days before suspending the account.
- A grace period of **14 days** is given after suspension before data deletion.

## Cancellation

- Accounts can be cancelled at any time from **Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription**.
- Cancellation is effective at the end of the current billing period — the customer retains access until then.
- A **data export** option is available before cancellation under **Settings → Data → Export**.
- After cancellation, data is retained for **90 days** before permanent deletion.
